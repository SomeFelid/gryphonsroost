Human and Dragon, Anessa and Esia. Bonded at birth, but never did they meet. Now that Anessa has escaped the control of the Domed City and met her bonded, its time for everything to change. Anessa is looking for the truth of her birth father, Esia is looking for who she really is, and their searches will lead to maybe the biggest truth of all. That is if Anessa isn't too distracted by the attractive courier she just met, and a tragedy in the Domed City doesn't put everything on hold.

Setting: Tinaswa Galaxy
