Gryphon is a nonbinary author and artist who goes by the *nom de plume* of Gryphon E. Schmidt. Contents of works include but are not limited to - adventure, queer folks, dragons, aliens, shape shifters, magic, science, found family, and romance.

Please use any pronoun for Gryphon as long is it is not he, she, or it. What does that leave left? [I'm so glad you asked…](http://www.pronouns.failedslacker.com/?subjective=ey\&object=em\&possDet=eir\&possPro=eirs\&reflexive=eirself\&name=Gryphon\&person=enban#pronounSelect)

Gryphon is not currently open for art or writing commissions, but does have stickers and prints of their work available, please see the contacts page for email and [fediverse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fediverse) contact options.
