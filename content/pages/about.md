Gryphon is a nonbinary author and artist who goes by the *nom de plume* of Gryphon E. Schmidt. Please use any pronoun for Gryphon as long is it is *not* he, she, or it. What does that leave left? [I'm so glad you asked…](http://www.pronouns.failedslacker.com?subjective=ey\&object=em\&possDet=eir\&possPro=eirs\&reflexive=eirself\&name=Gryphon\&person=enban#pronounSelect)

Contents of works include but are not limited to - adventure, queer folks, dragons, aliens, shape shifters, magic, science, family of many kinds, and romance. Sometimes Gryph will write things that contain sensitive subjects, like violence, but this will always be labeled. If this still bothers you, please consider reading something else! Now is a great time to discover the wide world that is indie authors.

Visual art should always have Alt text for screen readers, please get in touch if one seems to be missing or broken in any way.

Gryphon is not currently open for art or writing commissions, but does have stickers and prints of their work available, please see the contacts page for email and [fediverse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fediverse) contact options.
